Asta from Black Clover

the boy with no magic

  • Asta along with his Yuno are orphans who together were left under the care of a church during infancy who have mutual aspirations and rivalry of becoming the next Wizard King.
  • Asta generally is cheerful and hyperactive. He tends to shout to express his thoughts and goals, much to the annoyance of some of those around him, and often in spite of other people's opinions. Although Asta possesses no magical power, he overcame his weakness by increasing his physical strength, which allowed him to join the Magic Knight Squad known as the Black Bulls after acquiring a five-leaf clover grimoire.
  • The grimoire allows him to use Anti-Magic effectively due to his lack of mana, later honing his senses to predict an opponent's attack by sensing their qi.
